## 2. Logo Usage & Trust Signals

### Primary Logo
- **Format**: Full color logo with icon and wordmark
- **Usage**: Website header, official documents, presentations
- **Minimum Size**: 120px width for digital, 30mm for print
- **Clear Space**: Minimum 20px padding around logo

### Logo Variations
1. **Horizontal Logo**: Primary usage for headers and navigation
2. **Stacked Logo**: For square spaces (social media, app icons)
3. **Icon Only**: For favicon, app icons, watermarks
4. **Monochrome Gold**: For dark backgrounds
5. **Monochrome Navy**: For light backgrounds

### Logo Don'ts
- Never stretch or distort the logo
- Never change logo colors outside approved palette
- Never add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Never place on busy backgrounds
- Never rotate the logo

### Trust Signals to Display

#### On Homepage (Above the Fold)
1. **Bank Negara Malaysia Recognition**
   - "Recognized by Bank Negara Malaysia"
   - Display BNM logo with acknowledgment

2. **Insurance Partnership**
   - "Insurance-Backed Financing Solutions"
   - Partner insurer logos (where permitted)

3. **Industry Memberships**
   - "Member of Malaysian Insurance & Takaful Brokers Association (MITBA)"
   - "Member of Persatuan Insurans Am Malaysia (PIAM)"

4. **Trust Badges**
   - SSL Security Badge
   - "Secure & Confidential"
   - "Licensed & Regulated"

#### Footer Trust Elements
- Company registration number: "Company No. [XXXXXX-X]"
- "Licensed Financial Services Provider"
- "© 2025 Shefa Risk Management Sdn Bhd. All rights reserved."

## 5. Detailed Services List

### Service 1: Protection Against Non-Payment

**Overview**: Comprehensive insurance-backed protection that safeguards businesses against customer defaults, ensuring cash flow stability and financial security.

**Key Features**:
- Trade Credit Insurance: Protection against buyer non-payment
- Political Risk Coverage: Safeguard against government actions
- Commercial Risk Protection: Coverage for insolvency and protracted default
- Multi-Buyer Coverage: Protect entire customer portfolio
- Single Buyer Coverage: Targeted protection for key accounts

**Benefits**:
- Protect up to 90% of invoice value
- Expand sales to new customers with confidence
- Access better financing terms with insured receivables
- Reduce bad debt reserves
- Improve cash flow predictability

**Ideal For**: Manufacturers, exporters, B2B service providers, wholesalers

---

### Service 2: Policy Negotiation

**Overview**: Expert negotiation services to secure optimal insurance terms, ensuring comprehensive coverage at competitive rates.

**Key Features**:
- Market Analysis: Review of available insurance products
- Coverage Optimization: Tailor policies to business requirements
- Premium Negotiation: Secure competitive pricing
- Terms Improvement: Negotiate better deductibles and limits
- Multi-Insurer Programs: Structure complex coverage

**Process**:
1. Assessment: Analyze current coverage and identify gaps
2. Market Research: Review offerings from multiple insurers
3. Proposal Development: Create tailored recommendations
4. Negotiation: Secure optimal terms and pricing
5. Implementation: Facilitate policy placement

---

### Service 3: Claim Process Management

**Overview**: End-to-end claims management services that maximize recovery and minimize disruption when insured events occur.

**Key Features**:
- Claim Preparation: Compile documentation and evidence
- Filing Support: Submit claims correctly and promptly
- Negotiation: Advocate for maximum valid recovery
- Dispute Resolution: Handle claim disagreements
- Recovery Tracking: Monitor claim status and payments

**Claim Types**: Non-payment, insolvency, protracted default, political risk, pre-shipment risk

**Process**:
1. Immediate Response: 24-hour claim acknowledgment
2. Documentation: Gather all required evidence
3. Submission: File complete claim package
4. Follow-up: Regular status updates and advocacy
5. Recovery: Ensure prompt and full payment
